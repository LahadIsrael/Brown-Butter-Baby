Meet the Baker

Hi, I'm Adiva — baker, wife, mom, and founder of Brown Butter Baby. It all started in my kitchen, making cinnamon rolls for the people I love most. When I saw how much joy a warm, homemade treat could bring, I knew I had to share it. Brown Butter Baby is built on that same feeling: food made with intention, love, and no shortcuts. Proudly baking out of Georgetown, TX.
