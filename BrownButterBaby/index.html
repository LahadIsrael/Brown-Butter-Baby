Who are we?

Brown Butter Baby is a home bakery rooted in love, warmth, and the joy of baking from scratch. Every cinnamon roll, and sweat treat that comes out of our kitchen is made with carefully chosen ingredients and a whole lot of heart. We believe that homemade goods carry something special - the kind of comfort and care that you just can't find on a store shelf. From the first swirl of dough to the last drizzle of icing, we take pride in every single bite we share with you. Welcome to our kitchen!
